Projects

R3Filler

R3Filler is a mobile application which helps the users to save the planet by using their own water bottles and coffee cups and not using plastics. It helps user to find the refill stations to their own water bottles and coffee cups near them. With -1 use of plastic, User will earn points and after a certain number of points they will get something free. One more great feature of this app is user can see how many bottles they saved and how much money they saved from that.

Cake Candle Blower

The Cake Candle Blower is a fun and interactive virtual birthday celebration project. Using your microphone, this web application allows you to blow into the mic to virtually blow out candles on a cake. It's an innovative and playful way to celebrate birthdays online when you can’t be physically together. As you blow into the mic, the candles flicker and eventually blow out, giving you a realistic birthday experience. It even congratulates you once all the candles are blown out, making your virtual birthday celebration truly special.
